My name is Brooke Borzymowski, and I am a sophomore Broadcast and Digital Journalism major at Syracuse University. I am from Fallston, Maryland, where I live with my mom, dad, sister, and dog. My sister Rachel is a senior at Boston University, where she majors in Biology and minors in Art and Psychology. Most of all, I love spending time with my family, so anything with them is my activity of choice!
